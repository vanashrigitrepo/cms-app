About CMS

The Complaint Management System (CMS) is a web-based platform designed to help communities, organizations, and municipalities handle complaints efficiently and transparently. Whether it's infrastructure issues, service delays, or general feedback, our system ensures that every complaint is logged, tracked, and resolved in a structured workflow.

With CMS, citizens can submit complaints easily, and authorities can assign, monitor, and resolve issues quickly. This leads to greater transparency, faster responses, and increased public satisfaction.

Key Features

Easy Complaint Submission

Users can submit complaints easily through an intuitive web form with category and description.

Real-Time Tracking

Track complaint status live—pending, in progress, or resolved—at every stage of the workflow.

Admin Assignment

Admins can assign complaints to relevant departments or officers for timely action and resolution.

Analytics & Reports

Gain insights through analytics like top complaint categories, resolution time, and trends.
